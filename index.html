Understanding Your IQ Score

IQ scores typically follow a normal distribution, also known as a "bell curve," with:

  • 68% of scores falling between 85 and 115
  • 95% of scores falling between 70 and 130
  • 99.7% of scores falling between 55 and 145

The average (mean) IQ score is set at 100, with a standard deviation of 15 points.
